Charge Blade

One of the best weapon in the game. If you want to hunt monsters as fast as possible, this is the weapon for you. This weapon stocks charges everytime a monster is hit and can discharge it for increase attack damage. It can be difficult to get use to it but once you get the hang of it, it can minimise your hunting speed.

Great Sword

Great swords are very powerful weapons with a large damage cap that specialises in charge attacks which maximises your damage output. Due to its size, it is a very slow weapon but compensates on the amount of damage you can make for a three consecutive charge attacks and can also be used as a shield with the cost of your stamina.

Bow

A mid-range weapon that fires arrows to attack. Bow can be coated with different types of coating such as elemental coatings which increases your damage and status effect coatings that causes abnormal status to monsters. Charge attacks are also possible to maximise damage output.

Long Sword

Long swords are more agile and quicker compared to great swords but cannot be used as shield during those crucial moments when a monster attacks which takes us to its next benefit the counter slash. When timed correctly it can deflect monster attacks. Long sword can also be charged by each attack which increases weapon damage depending on the glow color.

Insect Glaive

Insect glaive specialises on aerial attacks which is important if you are trying to mount a monster. It has a fluid attack combo on both ground and aerial. It also consist of a kinsect that can be sent to a target to collect essence that increases your attributes depending on the color collected and green essence that slightly restore your health.

Dual Blade

Dual blades specialises in swift slashing combo attacks. If paired with evade plus three, evade distance and and mega juice that stops stamina depletion when on Archdemon mode allows you to be able to chain long and powerful combos.

Hammer

Hammer is a blunt weapon that is able to stun monsters. Just like the great sword it is capable of charge attacks that increases its damage upon impact. In a slope, it is capable of an aerial spinning attack that can cause monsters to be mounted. Note: If your in a party with a hammer user they usually target the head of a monster so leave them in charge in that area.
