Hunting

  • Lookout for patterns on how monsters attack and dodge or defend accordingly. Once you get used to a monster's movements then it will be easier to land attacks in a precise moment.
  • When in party, keep a close watch to each member's health and use lifepowder during emergencies.
  • Hammer users tend to aim for a monster's head, leave that area to them.
  • When a monster is put to sleep or sleeping, don't attack it some party members might put bombs that will increase its damage when set off.
  • Keep an eye on monsters when it's weakened and it's ready to be captured.
  • Don't farm crystals, herbs etc during a hunt. There are plenty of time doing that in solo play.
  • Please do not idle during a hunt.

Useful Tips

  • Combining items is very important learn how to do it. Combination List avalable HERE.
  • Monster elemental weakness can be checked on their armour sets. If it has a negative value, this represents their elemental weakness.
  • Keep checking weapon trees from online source such as THIS before crafting your weapons.
    It will give you a more detailed idea on what the final outcome of each weapon types.
  • For new players, to unlock armour abilities each skill should total up to ten which is level 1 so doing the math is not so bad and for some armour skills, if it is available it can go up to level 5 to unlock the maximum amount.
